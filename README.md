Program Door To Heaven - The Taylor Series
==========================================

This is a StarGate fan fiction.
The premise is that an N.I.D. employee (Taylor Jones)
is going through all available documents about the StarGate program.

I'm aiming to:

* Provide a brand-new point of view to the old stories we all love.
  But keep that compatible with the SG canon.
    * This will be challenging with the time-travel parts.
      I mean most of them didn't happen.
* Slightly educate on various topics and broaden the reader's horizons.
* Stay in-character - I'm writing this 30 years after the events,
  so I have to check what knowledge is too modern and what Taylor Jones could have known back then.
    * Like for example, I wanted to compare hieroglyphs to emojis in 1996, but the first set of emojis was from 1999.
* Make as many Indiana Jones crossovers as possible.
    * This gets difficult outside the 1930s.
      Suggestions wanted.
    * There will also be Easter Eggs for other fandoms.
      Like math...
* Be funny or at least entertaining.
    * Primarily for myself, but hopefully for you as well.

My goal is not quantity, but rather enjoying the process.
So there might be just a few episodes released per year.
This also depends on how many other side projects I currently have and how much I enjoy them.

If you have any suggestions or typo/grammar fixes, don't hesitate to contact me
in [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/ProgramDoorToHeaven/DoorToHeaven-TaylorSeries/issues).

You can start reading on [the web](https://programdoortoheaven.github.io/DoorToHeaven-TaylorSeries/index.html).
The `README.md` files on [GitHub](docs) should have the same contents, just without the author's comments.
In the web version, you can show or hide the comments using the corresponding buttons.
There are also some work-in-progress parts and notes.
If it is not accessible by links in the web version, consider it not published (yet).

Visit the [GateRoom](https://programdoortoheaven.github.io/) for more content.
